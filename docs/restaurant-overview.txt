DE GOUDEN LEPEL - RESTAURANT OPERATIONS OVERVIEW

About
De Gouden Lepel is a Dutch restaurant serving traditional and modern Dutch cuisine. The restaurant operates across multiple sales channels: dine-in, delivery (via platforms like Uber Eats, Thuisbezorgd), and takeaway.

Menu Categories
- Starters: Bitterballen, Kibbeling, Bruschetta Trio, Carpaccio van Rund, Hollandse Garnalen Cocktail, Kaasplank, Truffel Arancini
- Soups: Erwtensoep, Tomatensoep, Vissoep van de Dag
- Main Courses: Biefstuk, Ossenhaas, Stamppot Boerenkool, Stoverij, Kippenpoot uit de Oven, Lamsbout, Eendenborst, Truffel Risotto
- Fish & Seafood: Zalm Filet, Mosselen, Gebakken Schol, Tonijnsteak, Kreeft
- Vegetarian & Vegan: Aubergine Parmigiana, Pasta Primavera, Risotto ai Funghi, Pompoen Curry, Bloemkool Steak, Beyond Burger
- Lunch Specials: Broodje Kroket, Club Sandwich, Tosti Kaas & Ham, Uitsmijter, Poke Bowl Zalm, Poke Bowl Tofu, Salade Nicoise
- Desserts: Appeltaart met Slagroom, Stroopwafel Tiramisu, Creme Brulee, Chocolade Moelleux, Dame Blanche, Panna Cotta, Sorbet Trio
- Children's Menu: Kipnuggets met Friet, Kinderpannenkoek, Mini Frikandel met Friet, Pasta Bolognese, Mini Pannenkoekjes, Kinderijsje
- Side Dishes: Hand-Cut Frites, Truffel Frites, Aardappelpuree, Seizoensgroenten, Gemengde Salade
- Drinks: Heineken, La Chouffe, wines, cocktails (Amsterdam Mule, Gouden Lepel Spritz, Espresso Martini), coffee, tea, soft drinks

Departments
- Kitchen (head chef, sous chefs, line cooks)
- Front-of-House (servers, hosts, bartenders)
- Management

Key Suppliers
- Groenteboer van Dam (produce: aardappelen, uien, boerenkool, tomaten, sla, pompoen)
- Slagerij de Wit (meat: biefstuk, ossenhaas, kippenpoten, lamsbout, eendenborst, rookworst)
- Vishandel Noordzee (seafood: zalm, mosselen, schol, garnalen)
- Zuivelhoeve (dairy: boter, room, kaas, eieren)
- Groothandel Bakker (pantry: meel, rijst, pasta, erwten)
- Mediterrane Import (olijfolie)

Operational Priorities
1. Keep top-selling items fully stocked at all times
2. Monitor labor costs vs revenue (target: under 30% ratio)
3. Minimize waste and spoilage
4. Ensure adequate staffing during peak hours
5. Track channel performance to optimize delivery platform partnerships
